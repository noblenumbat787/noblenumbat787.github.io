2000s

Universal Design

Introduced in 2001, Universal Design saw a redesign of the buttons - they were now barrel-shaped; plus a change in typeface to Gill Sans from Helvetica. Also, the car position indicators saw a redesign as well.

These fixtures were used on Elenessa as well as NexWay lifts during the 2000s. The smaller buttons for high-rise installations also saw some changes; including the shift to Gill Sans as its typeface, as well as the change in shape of the door control and alarm buttons to match Universal Design.

ELEMOTION

ELEMOTION is Mitsubishi's solution for lift modernisations, having been introduced in 2001, replacing prior modernisation solutions such as the GPS-IIIMZ.
These lifts are generally modernisations of older Mitsubishi lifts only; as they cannot be used for modernisations of non-Mitsubishi lifts (in which case a full replacement is required).
Originally, ELEMOTION was meant for low or mid-rise buildings only; for high-rise modernisation the NexWay Modernization was offered instead.

ELEMOTION lifts modernised during this era generally came with Universal Design fixtures; and generally used an altered version of the regular two-tone Mitsubishi chime as well.
